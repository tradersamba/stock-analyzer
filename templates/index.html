What is P/E Ratio?

The P/E ratio compares a company's stock price to its earnings per share.

P/E = Stock Price ÷ EPS

A higher P/E may mean investors expect stronger future growth, while a lower P/E may suggest the stock is cheaper compared to earnings.

This analyzer compares the company's P/E ratio against similar peer companies to estimate whether the stock appears undervalued, fairly valued, or overvalued.
